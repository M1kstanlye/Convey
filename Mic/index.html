Welcome to Convey!

what exactly do we do?

Convey is a website/app dedicated to ending the food waste epidemic in America. Here we provide a donation funded service which allows people to help reduce their own and america's over all food waste.

Our services provide people with the ability to donate wasted food track to get rid of food that is or going bad. Wasted food is the #1 coupent of our landfills. This shouldn't be the case. In a world where America could literally feed every mouth in its own population, but still has loads of people on the street starving is very sad and can be prevented.
